“Part of it”

This concept represents a boy getting list in the memory of his ex. It represents the singers continual love for his ex and the struggle he is having to completely accept that she is gone. He has already come to terms with the breakup but her memory still permeates his mind. This concept represents the hopeful attitude that the singer has toward the whole ordeal. He chooses to accept that she is gone, but still finds the memories of their relationship to be pleasant. Without those memories he wouldn’t be what he his today.
