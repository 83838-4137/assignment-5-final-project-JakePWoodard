“I'm Jake Woodard, a graphic designer merging traditional and digital media to craft clever concepts and captivating designs.”

Why I Design

From the strokes of a paintbrush to the precision of pixels, I thrive on diversifying my craft and learning something new with every project. My designs are not just visual representations; they're stories waiting to be told, emotions waiting to be felt, and ideas waiting to be embraced. I provide design solutions to your problems and even answer problems you didn't even know you had.

How I design

As a self-proclaimed art and book nerd I find joy in delivering on concepts that find clever alternatives to the obvious. For me design is like reading a good book or telling a story, there has to be a good 3 act structure with a strong resolution or it becomes mundane. And who wants that? No matter the reason there is always a story to tell through your designs.
